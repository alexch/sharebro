Missing: 'Note in Reader'

Original Google Reader

  • bookmarklet
  • adds an item to the user's shared items feed
  • grabs the current page's title and URL
  • grabs a relevant excerpt of the current page (excluding extraneous content as if by magic)
  • selection-aware: if the user has selected a portion of the page, uses that selection as the excerpt
  • all fields are editable prior to submission

Replacement: Share on Tumblr

  • adds an item to the user's tumblr blog
  • grabs the current page's title and URL
  • selection-aware
  • con: tumblr has many odd posting formats (and chooses arbitrarily between 'quote' and 'link' and 'discussion' and 'image')
  • con: tumblr loses HTML and image formatting from excerpt

Missing: Shared Items Feed

Replacement: ridllr

  • Ridllr.com
  • pulls in your old 'people you follow' feeds and resubscribes you to them
  • con: many names are lost and replaced with long numbers

Replacement: lipsumarium's userscripts

  • Google Reader Share -- serves feeds off of lipsumarium.com -- we'll probably roll this into sharebro.org soon

Missing: Share button

Original Google Reader

  • in 'action bar' while viewing a single item in Reader
  • adds that item to the user's shared items feed

Replacement: Google Reader Share

    http://lipsumarium.com/
  • Google Reader Share
  • adds a 'Share' button the the top action bar in Reader
  • also adds a 'add friends by email' box in the left sidebar in Reader
